Interactive Typography

White Box

For my project, I wanted to use machine learning api to to detect and map facial features then duplicate/ swap detected subjects.
Additionally, ideal output would be in the style of cubism.



Process

I used Jupyter Notebook and OpenCV (both are relatively new to me)
I initially wrote a script just for camera testing w/ black & white filter.



I then played around with defining alll object outlines within camera for a canny edge effect.



After that, facial recognition was implemented and was able to detect multiple faces.
